purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase my Lights Academy capstone project as well as function as a web portfolio to highlight the projects I have worked on throughout highschool. While making this website, I have documented my steps and what I learned through the process.

and objective.

My objective was to create a website that is both visually appealing and functional. I wanted it to be minimalistic and easy to navigate without sacrificing any content.
